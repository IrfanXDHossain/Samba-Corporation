About Samba Corporation

Origins

Samba Corporation traces its origins back to the visionary entrepreneur, Samiul Islam Inam, in the heart of Dhaka, Bangladesh. In 2010, driven by a passion for Different Uses, Samiul established a small workshop, laying the foundation for what would become a global enterprise.

Early Years and Growth

The early years were marked by innovation and unwavering dedication. Samiul introduced groundbreaking services with manufacturing electronics with their own company Samba Electronics, that revolutionized the manufacturing sector. This pioneering spirit, coupled with a relentless pursuit of quality, propelled Samba to rapid growth. As word of their exceptional offerings spread, demand surged, leading to the expansion of operations and the establishment of new facilities.

A Global Presence

Recognizing the immense potential of international markets, Samba embarked on an ambitious globalization strategy. Through strategic partnerships and meticulous market analysis, the company successfully expanded its reach to key regions across the globe. Today, Samba boasts a vast network of offices and manufacturing plants, catering to a diverse clientele spanning continents.
